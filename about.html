About LoveU Calc

Fast. Private. Simple.

LoveU Calc was built to solve a simple problem: most online calculator websites are slow, cluttered with ads, and difficult to use on mobile phones.

We believe that when you need to calculate a grade, a mortgage payment, or a unit conversion, you shouldn't have to wait for 5MB of scripts to load. You want the answer, and you want it now.

Our Core Principles

  • Zero Bloat: Our pages are usually under 15KB. We use no heavy frameworks or tracking scripts.
  • Privacy First: Calculations happen on your device (in your browser). We do not send your data to our servers.
  • Mobile First: Every tool is designed for touchscreens first, ensuring large buttons and readable text on small screens.

Who is this for?

LoveU Calc is for students, professionals, and homeowners who value efficiency. Whether you are calculating your CGPA for university applications or estimating concrete for a driveway, our tools are free to use forever.
