How to Do Long Division (The 4 Steps)

In the US, students remember the cycle using the mnemonic "Does McDonald's Sell Burgers?":

Step 1: Divide (Daddy)

Check how many times the divisor fits into the first digit of the dividend.

Step 2: Multiply (Mommy)

Multiply that number by the divisor and write the result underneath.

Step 3: Subtract (Sister)

Subtract your result from the digit above to find the remainder.

Step 4: Bring Down (Brother)

Bring down the next digit from the dividend and start the cycle again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF Worksheet generator free?

Yes! It is completely free. Teachers and parents can generate unlimited worksheets for practice without any registration or cost.

What do I do if the remainder is not zero?

You can write it as "R" (e.g., 5 R1) for school homework, or toggle the "Decimal" mode in our tool to continue dividing until you get a precise decimal answer.

What is Access Mode (Dyscalculia)?

We added a special feature for students with learning differences. By clicking the "Access Mode" checkbox, the tool changes to high-contrast colors and adds grid lines to help keep numbers aligned visually.
